The Best Movies According to Steve

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Kid

I love the story of a young kid's life and the opportunity for redemption after the negative impacts of family brokenness.

The Fugitive

The main characters of this movie, Harrison Ford and Tommy Lee Jones, show brilliance and ingenuity with perseverance under great difficulty. Very enjoyable if you like suspense movies.

National Treasure

This movie has great characters with delightful chemistry. The historical basis of the story and the conflict of good and evil battling throughout the movie is the stuff of legends.
